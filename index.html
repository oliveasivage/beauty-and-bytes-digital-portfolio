💄 Sephora Foundation Equity Analysis

💡 Research Question

Are Sephora foundation products equally serving customers across different skin tones?

🌍 Project Overview

This project analyzes 379 customer reviews scraped from Sephora’s top foundation products. The goal is to evaluate inclusivity and performance across skin tone and skin type using statistical methods.
